Who am I?
After being born in the US, I lived in Great Britain, France, Greece, Sweden, Australia, and today in Germany. Growing into adulthood I kept on travelling in many different parts of the World which brought me a lot of amazing experiences and memories.
My interest for music started at the age of 8. I learned to play several instruments, and played in several bands in Paris.
I graduated of a Master degree in International Finance at Université Paris Nanterre and found a job as a statistician in an insurance company. At the same time I started composing for films and television, such as the French-German channel Arte.
I got married and my wife and I moved to Berlin in 2008 to start a new life. We opened a fashion shop In Prenzl'berg, before I dedicated my full time to music and sound design. I have had a succesful career in the music industry for 10 years, before I wanted a new challenge in my life. This new challenge is coding.
